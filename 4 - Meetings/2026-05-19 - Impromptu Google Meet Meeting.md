## Topics

### Account Enrichment Process

- Current process: Verify name, address, phone via website/LinkedIn/ZoomInfo → Update Salesforce → Research industries and employee count.
- **Problem:** ZoomInfo data is collected by overseas callers and rarely updated. One account was listed as $1M in Salesforce, $10M in ZoomInfo, but was actually $40M.

### New Call Strategy: Conversational Framing

- **Revenue Talk Track:** "The last time we connected, you were at $17.9M. Has that gone up or down?" — makes the call feel like a follow-up, not a cold call.
- **Software Talk Track:** "I talk to landscapers all day, and QuickBooks is common for accounting. What other software do you use?" — acknowledging common software shows industry knowledge and lowers resistance.

### New Call Strategy: Playful Mindset & Pattern Interrupts

- Unexpected openers: "Oh my God, I didn't think you'd answer!" or "If I told you this was a cold call, would you throw your phone out the window?"
- Personal questions: "What's your favorite Starbucks order?"
- Referencing past interactions: "I remember you said every time you walk in, something goes wrong. Has that happened yet today?"

### Outage Talk Track

- Deploy the "what's your plan for July?" question only after confirming: (1) prospect uses a competitor (e.g., Jobber), and (2) they were impacted by a past outage.
