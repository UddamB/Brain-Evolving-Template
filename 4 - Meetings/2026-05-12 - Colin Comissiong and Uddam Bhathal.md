## Topics

### The "Become the Bank" Strategy

  - [**Problem:** Traditional development is slow (7-year cycles) and capital-intensive, forcing reliance on risky second-tier lenders.](https://fathom.video/share/UohW2DWTCt6xm9BHKMQeC6NS2g3ysB5h?tab=summary&timestamp=1472.0)
  - [**Solution:** A nimble model focused on smaller projects (30–40 units) with rapid 18-month cycles.](https://fathom.video/share/UohW2DWTCt6xm9BHKMQeC6NS2g3ysB5h?tab=summary&timestamp=1517.0)
  - [**Process:**](https://fathom.video/share/UohW2DWTCt6xm9BHKMQeC6NS2g3ysB5h?tab=summary&timestamp=1179.0)
    1.  [**Acquire:** Source off-market properties (e.g., via CoStar) by offering owners value (e.g., tax mitigation).](https://fathom.video/share/UohW2DWTCt6xm9BHKMQeC6NS2g3ysB5h?tab=summary&timestamp=326.0)
    2.  [**Carry:** Secure front-end capital to cover operational costs during development.](https://fathom.video/share/UohW2DWTCt6xm9BHKMQeC6NS2g3ysB5h?tab=summary&timestamp=379.0)
    3.  [**Exit:** Refinance with top-tier banks (e.g., Royal Bank) using CMHC financing. This allows pulling out up to 95% of equity, which is then reinvested to acquire the next property, creating a self-funding loop.](https://fathom.video/share/UohW2DWTCt6xm9BHKMQeC6NS2g3ysB5h?tab=summary&timestamp=1179.0)
