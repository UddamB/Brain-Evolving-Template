### Practice Overview & Bottlenecks

- **Focus:** Families and small business owners.
- **Primary Bottleneck:** Lead generation — paid services are expensive ($8k+) and often unqualified.

### Client Acquisition & Onboarding

- Channels: Word-of-mouth and BNI referrals.
- Onboarding: Qualifying questions → Scheduling (offer limited time options) → Application pre-fill (~80% done) → DocuSign + remote screen share for sensitive data.

### Client Education & Retention

- All education is live and interactive (one-on-one or group). Pre-recorded video is ineffective.
- Key concepts: Rule of 72, 76% positive market history, cost of market timing.
- Retention: Quarterly contact + annual in-person review. Differentiator vs. low-contact competitors.
- Follow-up: Primerica CRM drip campaigns + monthly check-in for unresponsive prospects.

### Small Business Strategy

- **Value Prop:** Offer employee benefits at no cost to the business (individual life insurance boosts retention).
- **401(k) vs. Oregon Saves:** Oregon Saves = simple Roth IRA with no employer match, blocks employees from opening outside Roth IRA. 401(k) = employer matching + 3-year federal tax credit for 100% of setup costs.
- **Referral Strategy:** Educate CPAs/attorneys on complex plans (Solo K) to help their clients save on taxes → build referral relationships.
