# Weekly review

Run once a week. Goal: compress the week into signal and make sure the next week is pointed at the right things.

## 1. Map the week's landscape
Before synthesizing, build a mental map:
- What topics consumed the most energy this week?
- What emotions were most present?
- What decisions were made or avoided?
- What moved forward? What stayed stuck?
- What surprised me or broke a pattern?

## 2. Process the live logs
- Re-read `ACTIONS_LOG`, `DECISIONS_LOG`, `EMOTIONS_LOG`, `LEARNING_LOG`, `REFLECTIONS_LOG` for the week.

## 3. Compare short-term against long-term
This is the core analytical move. Read recent reflections against the bigger picture:
- Cross-reference: are any core patterns showing evidence of evolution — something that used to be true and isn't anymore?
- Cross-reference: are recurring emotional patterns being handled differently than they were a month ago?
- Are active projects still aligned with `1 - Aspirations/GOALS.md`?
- Does anything in the week challenge `0 - Identity/SOUL.md`?

## 4. Update forward-looking docs
- Adjust `ACTIVE PROJECTS`, `GOALS`, and `HABITS` if reality has moved.
- Flag anything in Identity that may need a conscious revision (never auto-edit).

## 5. Update North Star metrics
Append one row to `8 - North Star/METRICS_LOG.md`:
- **Step-away hours** (median/day this week): longest uninterrupted unreachable stretch, median over 7 days.
- **Automation %**: list the top ~20 tasks I actually did this week, tag each A / G / M, compute (A + G) / total.
- **Revenue / person (TTM)**: refresh monthly; carry the last value forward other weeks and mark `(carry)`.
- One-sentence note on what moved and why.
Then sanity-check: is the loop (automation → step-away → revenue/person) still working, or is one metric drifting alone? If so, flag it in the next week's focus.

## 6. Set the next week's focus
- Top 3 outcomes for next week.
- One thing to stop doing.
- If a North Star metric is drifting, one move to correct it.
